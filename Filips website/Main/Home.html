Flora of Macedonia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The flora of the Republic of Macedonia is represented with around 210 families, 920 genera, and around 3,700 plant species. The most abundant group are the flowering plant with around 3,200 species, wich is folowed by mosses (350 species) and ferns (42).
Phytogeography, Macedonia belongs to the Illyrian of the Circumboreal Region within the Boreal Kingdom. According to the WWF and Digital Map of European Ecological Regions by the European Environment Agency, the territory of the Republic ca be subdivided into four ecoregions: the Pindus Mountains mixed forests, Balkan mixed forests, Rhodopes mixed forests, and Agean sclerophyllous and mixed forests.
